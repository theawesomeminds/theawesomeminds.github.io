Concocting the VA box

The  pretense here is to construct an exemplary box that serves as a paragon  of ostentation . It requires all the right ingredients: Jarus, his  wrath ,ravenous thirst for blogging,some feigning and a plethora of  bombardment. But the impeccable VA box can be conceived only with  bountiful use of vocabulary with a robust ascendancy over the English  grammar.And to read this diadem of a vignette with sheer eloquence is  tricky.You must have the Tyler attitude along with Jarus's VA English  accent.Ineffable sense of joy !
