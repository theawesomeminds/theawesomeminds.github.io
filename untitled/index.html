(Untitled)

Somewhere in the zillions of years of time spanning across zillions  of space filled with void and dust, somewhere in a tiny spot, some dust  acquired consciousness. That's us. There's infinity encompassing us, in  time, and in space. What you look like today, what you wear today, and  what you do today makes a teeny tiny difference to this planet, and no  difference to this universe and beyond.
